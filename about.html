About me

Mattie Balagat (she/her) is a third-year Filipino university student somewhere in Japan. (Not Tokyo!) She is somewhat set on exploring environmental anthropology in the Philippines to figure out futures where we collectively survive climate change. She does PR for Wavefarers, a Filipino youth environmental organization. She is also an intern for Agam Agenda and the Global Atlas of Environmental Justice.

She calls herself an artist but comes out with poetry and lettering very sporadically. It's frustrating, personally.

She is always craving cheese. Please fund her craving by buying merch and supporting her on Ko-fi!
